Overview

Purpose

To help young women and leaders to have a special site where they can have announcements, reminders of special dates, photos, and videos of the activities that they do, and they can access all the material and links that there are for LDS youth. Also, a place for leaders where they can also have special information for them to help them in their callings.

Audience

This page will be directed to the young women of the ward, their parents, and relatives, and their leaders as well as people who want to know a little more about what is being done in this organization.

Content

Home page

"We are daughters of a Heavenly Father who loves us and we love Him" The purpose of the Young Women organization is to guide and nurture young women as they become covenant women of God, as well as to help each young woman be worthy to make and keep sacred covenants and receive the ordinances and temple blessings. As presidency of the organization of young women of the RedBud Valley ward, we focus on helping each of the young women to fulfill this purpose through activities that we carry out and classes every Sunday. On this page you can find images and videos of these activities, but you can also find important information about content that the Church has available for youngs and their leaders. We hope you enjoy it and can find out a little more about what this organization does for each of our dear young women and what they also contribute to us.

Leaders

As young women leaders we have a great responsibility and an enormous privilege to guide and assist young women at this stage in their lives. The Church provides several resources that can be very useful to be able to exercise our calling in the best way and provides us with what is necessary to do so. Here we can find things like instruction manuals, class manuals, guides, lists, etc. which can help us magnify our calling. Each one of us is a very important link in this organization and we hope to be able to give you all the help you need. As the ward presidency, we greatly value your time and dedication and we thank you very much for your willingness to serve.
